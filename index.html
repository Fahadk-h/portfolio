Who am I

I’m Fahad Alhayyan, I work data then excavate insightful information for decision making. I am based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia and help with data cleaning, modeling, visualization, advance SQL an have solid knowledge of programming languages and structure.

Over the past year after graduation, I’ve helped develop prototypes, inhouse application solution, and implement data Analytics and BI, while I took courses to grow my analytical skills and built personal projects

I like to work out, play video games, grow plants and sometimes make content. Video games taught me focus and persistent while growing plants gave a better understanding that with patients and good work, I can support the people I work with reach goals.

Though I need my time to charge my social battery, I spend time reading books, deepening skills, watching sitcom and I trying very hard not to be a bad cook.
